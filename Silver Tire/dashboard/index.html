How to Use Dashboard

🚀 Quick Start

First Time Setup:
  1. Press Ctrl+Shift+R to hard refresh
  2. Click "Test" on Gmail card to test email
  3. Click "Test" on WhatsApp card (scan QR code first time)
  4. Click "Post" on LinkedIn card (login first time)

📧 Send Email

  1. Click "Test" button on Gmail card (sends test email)
  2. OR click "New Email" in Quick Actions (custom email)
  3. Fill in: To, Subject, Message
  4. Click "Send Email"
  5. Check your inbox at solemanseher@gmail.com

💬 Send WhatsApp

  1. Click "Test" button on WhatsApp card
  2. First time: Scan QR code with phone
  3. Message sends automatically
  4. OR use "Send WhatsApp" in Quick Actions for custom number

💼 Post to LinkedIn

  1. Click "Post" button on LinkedIn card
  2. First time: Login to LinkedIn
  3. Post publishes automatically
  4. OR use "Post to LinkedIn" in Quick Actions for custom post

🔍 View Logs

  • Click "Logs" button on any platform card
  • Opens audit log in new tab
  • Shows all sent emails/messages/posts
  • Check "Recent Activity" section for live feed

📊 Metrics

  • Emails Sent Today: Count of emails sent
  • WhatsApp Messages: Messages sent
  • LinkedIn Posts: Posts published
  • Total Actions: Combined count
  • Updates every 30 seconds automatically
✅ Tips:
  • Always hard refresh first time (Ctrl+Shift+R)
  • Check activity feed for confirmation
  • Logs button shows detailed history
  • Press F12 for console logs if issues
