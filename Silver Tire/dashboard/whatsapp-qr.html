📱 WhatsApp QR Scanner

Scan QR code to enable WhatsApp messaging

⚠️ FIRST TIME SETUP REQUIRED
You need to scan QR code with your phone

📋 How to Scan QR Code:

  1. Click "Open WhatsApp Web" button below
  2. Browser will open with WhatsApp Web
  3. QR code will appear on screen
  4. Take your phone and:
    • Open WhatsApp app
    • Go to Settings (iPhone) or ⋮ Menu (Android)
    • Tap Linked Devices
    • Tap Link a Device
    • Point phone camera at QR code on screen
  5. Wait for login (5-10 seconds)
  6. Check "Keep me signed in" if asked
  7. Close browser when done
