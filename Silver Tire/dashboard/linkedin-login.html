💼 LinkedIn Login Helper

Login to LinkedIn to enable autonomous posting

⚠️ FIRST TIME SETUP REQUIRED
You need to login to LinkedIn once

📋 How to Login:

  1. IMPORTANT: Use Google Chrome for login!
  2. Click "Open LinkedIn in Chrome" button below
  3. Chrome will open LinkedIn login page
  4. Enter your LinkedIn credentials in Chrome:
    • Email or Phone
    • Password
  5. Click "Sign in"
  6. If verification required, complete it
  7. Once on your feed, navigate around for 10 seconds
  8. Check "Stay signed in" if asked
  9. Keep Chrome open - don't close it
  10. Come back here and click "Test LinkedIn Post"
⚠️ IMPORTANT: You must login in Google Chrome, not Internet Explorer or Edge!
The automation uses Chrome, so the login session must be in Chrome.
